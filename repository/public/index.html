Objectives and Ideas

“Danielle Nicholas waited for eight months to be told that yes, she had cancer.” that's what the news was from Brisbane Times by Lucy Stone on June 2018. Recent rural healthcare system has given the country advantages and benefits. However, the system working now still have issues. One of them is the unstructured referral system where it brings time loss for a patient to see a specialist. Easy Referral is the platform to make the referral process easier and quicker. Easy Referral aims to organized referral process within all rural healthcares.

Social and Mobile

Social Interaction between GP, Physician, and patient is formed after the referral built connecting all parties. Location awareness while creating the referral is based on the nearest physician.

Collaborative Works

Referral is made with an agreement by both GP and patient. An appointment is approved by GP, patient and also Physician. Without one party involved in the process, neither referral and appointment will be arranged. Asynchronous communication chat platform is for GP and Physician.

Desktop & App

Desktop Website interfaces made are for GP and Physician, whereas Mobile Application is for Patient. The UI aims to be simple and clear for any age range to use. The UX aims to be adaptive in any devices possible and offer the simplest process.
